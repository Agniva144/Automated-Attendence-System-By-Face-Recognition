Guiding Details





    UPLOAD PHOTO:  

  • Here any new user have to upload their photo strictly in any of the following format specified : ['jpg', 'jpeg', 'png']. User also needs to focus on the fact that the name of the photo should match with the name of the user. (E.g- If your name is Suresh , you should upload the image by the name 'Suresh.jpg' or 'Suresh.jpeg' or 'Suresh.png').
    		•

      MARK ATTENDENCE:  

  • Once the photo is uploaded , we have your image with us. Now all you have to do is to click 'Mark Attendence' button which will use your system camera (which get prompt in another window) and just put your face in the frame and it will detect the person with his/her name below it. Once done press 'q' or 'Q' any of it to close it and return back to our website.
    		•

      DOWNLOAD CSV:  

  • This button when clicked will download a csv file having the record of the faces detected with Name,Date and Time for the same. This csv file can serve as a storing record for the number of faces detected during its time of working.
